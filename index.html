Avalanche Arena

Mint, Stake, and Battle your Arena Characters

Mint New Character

Mint a new Character NFT for 0.05 AVAX. New characters start with base stats: 100 STR, 100 AGI, 100 INT.

Character Staking

  • Staking locks your Character NFT in the Game Engine contract to make it stronger.
  • For every full 30-day period staked, your character's Strength increases by 1.
  • Agility and Intelligence remain at their base value (100).
  • You can unstake at any time, but strength is only gained for completed 30-day periods.
  • When you unstake, your character is returned to your wallet with its updated stats permanently recorded.
  • Staked characters cannot be transferred or used in combat.

Arena Combat

Enter two of your unstaked Character IDs to fight. Combat is resolved on-chain based on total power (STR*3 + AGI*2 + INT*1). Win/loss records are updated on the NFTs.
